First Onsite Experience and Continuing Journeys

An excellent first onsite experience is essential in capturing the user once they've arrived to avoid them immediately bouncing away. This is achieved by a combination of delivering the expected content in an engaging way and providing onward journeys that eliminate the need to leave the site.

Continuing journeys should allow consumers a route towards a point of conversion. For example, Expert Reviews (for "In Production" vehicles) this is either directly into the New Car Selector journey, or in the near future into Known Search to view Virtual Stock. Should the user not be ready to take this route, further exploratory/research options should be available. Finally, to avoid the need to bounce, an exit route a less specific section of the site needs to be available.
